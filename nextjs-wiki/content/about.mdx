# About

Learn more about this wiki and its purpose.

## Mission

This wiki is dedicated to sharing knowledge about recording, audio production, sounds, and related technologies.

## Content Areas

- **Recording**: Techniques, best practices, and equipment guides
- **Sounds**: Audio samples and sound design resources
- **Audio**: Audio processing and playback examples
- **Video**: Video production and playback techniques

## Technology

Built with Next.js 14, MDX, and Tailwind CSS for a modern, responsive experience.
