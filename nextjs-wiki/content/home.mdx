# Welcome to the Field Recording Library

This wiki is pre-seeded and ready to browse. No installer, no setup ---
just start exploring.

## Catalogs

- [Recordings](/recording/recording) --- gear checklist, setups, and
  file hygiene.
- [Sounds](/sounds/sounds) --- curated packs ready to drop into your
  sessions.
  - [Urban Ambience](/sounds/urban-ambience) --- beds, subway hums, city
    loops.
  - [Foley Essentials](/sounds/foley-essentials) --- cloth, paper,
    props, doors.

## Get Started

1.  Browse the [guide](/recording/recording) to see the recommended
    setup.
2.  Grab a pack from [Sounds](/sounds/sounds) to prototype your mix.
3.  Replace placeholder media in 'recording/' and 'sounds/' with your
    assets.
4.  Login with your admin account to edit; add demo users by starting
    with 'DEMO_USERS=1'.
