### What recording techniques are covered?
This guide covers comprehensive topics including:
- Microphone placement and polar patterns
- Signal flow and gain staging
- Room treatment and acoustic control
- File organization and metadata standards
- Genre-specific approaches

### Do you provide equipment recommendations?
**Yes!** Check out our **[Equipment Guide](/recording/equipment-guide)** and **[My Equipment](/recording/my-equipment)** sections for detailed reviews and recommendations.

### What about beginner tips?
Perfect place to start: **[Best Practices](/recording/best-practices)** covers foundational knowledge every audio professional needs.

### What recording formats do you recommend?
I recommend:
- **24-bit / 48kHz** minimum for professional work
- **24-bit / 96kHz** for maximum post-production flexibility
- **WAV format** for archival and editing
