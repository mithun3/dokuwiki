# Equipment

Essential equipment and tools for recording and audio production.

## Microphones

### Dynamic Microphones
- Shure SM7B - Industry standard for vocals and instruments
- Electro-Voice RE20 - Versatile dynamic microphone
- Rode Procaster - Broadcast quality dynamic

### Condenser Microphones
- Neumann U87 - Industry standard large diaphragm condenser
- Audio-Technica AT4050 - Multi-pattern condenser
- Rode NT1 - Budget-friendly large diaphragm condenser

## Audio Interfaces

- Universal Audio Apollo Twin
- Focusrite Scarlett Series
- RME Fireface UFX III

## Microphone Accessories

- Microphone stands and boom arms
- Pop filters and windscreens
- XLR cables (quality is important!)
- Shock mounts

## Headphones

- Sennheiser HD 660S - Open-back reference headphones
- Sony MDR-7506 - Closed-back studio standard
- Audio-Technica ATH-M70x - Professional monitoring

## Room Treatment

- Acoustic panels
- Bass traps
- Diffusers
- Foam corners

## Software

- DAW (Digital Audio Workstation)
- Audio editing software
- Plugins and effects
- Monitoring software
