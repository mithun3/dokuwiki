====== Welcome to the Field Recording Library ======

This wiki is pre-seeded and ready to browse. No installer, no setup — just start exploring.

==== Catalogs ====
  * [[recording:guide|recordings]] — gear checklist, setups, and file hygiene.
  * [[soundpacks:start|soundpacks]] — curated packs ready to drop into your sessions.
    * [[soundpacks:urban-ambience|Urban Ambience]] — beds, subway hums, city loops.
    * [[soundpacks:foley-essentials|Foley Essentials]] — cloth, paper, props, doors.

==== Highlights ====
  * Field kit quick-start: mic placement, wind control, and take naming in the guide.
  * Preview and swap in your own WAVs/MP3s for each pack page.
  * All visitors can read; admins can update and publish.

==== Get Started ====
  1. Browse the [[recording:guide|guide]] to see the recommended setup.
  2. Grab a pack from [[soundpacks:start|Sound Packs]] to prototype your mix.
  3. Replace placeholder media in :recording: and :soundpacks: with your assets.
  4. [[?do=login|Login]] with your admin account to edit; add demo users by starting with `DEMO_USERS=1`.

Need support for donations? Embed your PayPal button on any public page (client-side only).
