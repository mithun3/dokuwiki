====== Welcome to the Field Recording Library ======

This wiki is pre-seeded and ready to browse. No installer, no setup — just start exploring.

===== Catalogs =====

  * [[recording:recording|Recordings]] — gear checklist, setups, and file hygiene.
  * [[sounds:sounds|Sounds]] — curated packs ready to drop into your sessions.
    * [[sounds:urban-ambience|Urban Ambience]] — beds, subway hums, city loops.
    * [[sounds:foley-essentials|Foley Essentials]] — cloth, paper, props, doors.

===== Highlights =====

  * Field kit quick-start: mic placement, wind control, and take naming in the guide.
  * Preview and swap in your own WAVs/MP3s for each pack page.
  * All visitors can read; admins can update and publish.

===== Get Started =====

  - Browse the [[recording:recording|guide]] to see the recommended setup.
  - Grab a pack from [[sounds:sounds|Sounds]] to prototype your mix.
  - Replace placeholder media in 'recording/' and 'sounds/' with your assets.
  - Login with your admin account to edit; add demo users by starting with 'DEMO_USERS=1'.
