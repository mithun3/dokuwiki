# Welcome to the Field Recording Library

This wiki is pre-seeded and ready to browse. No installer, no setup — just start exploring.

## Catalogs

- [Recordings](recording/recording.md) — gear checklist, setups, and file hygiene.
- [Sounds](sounds/sounds.md) — curated packs ready to drop into your sessions.
  - [Urban Ambience](sounds/urban-ambience.md) — beds, subway hums, city loops.
  - [Foley Essentials](sounds/foley-essentials.md) — cloth, paper, props, doors.

## Highlights

- Field kit quick-start: mic placement, wind control, and take naming in the guide.
- Preview and swap in your own WAVs/MP3s for each pack page.
- All visitors can read; admins can update and publish.

## Get Started

1. Browse the [guide](recording/recording.md) to see the recommended setup.
2. Grab a pack from [Sounds](sounds/sounds.md) to prototype your mix.
3. Replace placeholder media in `recording/` and `sounds/` with your assets.
4. Login with your admin account to edit; add demo users by starting with `DEMO_USERS=1`.
